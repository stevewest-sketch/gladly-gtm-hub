	‚Ä¢	Sidekick's Core Architecture and Trust Layer Steve West explained that Sidekick's underlying architecture remains consistent, whether operating as a full suite within Gladly or as a standalone product. This architecture, termed "experience orchestration," enables accurate and trusted responses, secure transactions, and contextual escalations to third-party systems. The system utilizes the same knowledge sources, guides, and escalation rules and offers performance insights regardless of the platform. Steve West further elaborated on the "trust layer," a crucial component where every Sidekick response undergoes checks for tone, brand alignment, fact-checking, hallucination prevention, promise detection, compliance, and safety before reaching the customer. Responses must pass a confidence threshold, and if they fail twice, they are escalated to an agent, ensuring trustworthiness, brand consistency, and safety across all channels and platforms.
